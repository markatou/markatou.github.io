BIO

I am a second year Master student at MIT with a concentration in Theoretical Computer Science. Prior to starting my Masters, I earned my B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science with a minor in Mathemantics from MIT. My thesis is on theoretical distributed algorithms, supervised by Nancy Lynch. More specifically, I'm working on the SINR model, on various problems like leader election, broadcast, and node locating.

I am interested in design and analysis of algorithms, especially distributed algorithms. Additionally, I am very interested in cryptography and cryptanalysis.
